Day in the Life of an Idea

Getting the first idea out of your head and onto paper can be the hardest task...but once you do, the ideas just start flowing out.

You'll love some and hate many. Regardless, you have to keep writing no matter how weird or ridiculous they sound.

It's important to give the ideas some rest, and come back to them the next day.

Once you come back with a fresh set of eyes, the clear winners will stand out.

It may have been the first idea you had or the last you squeezed out. It stood up to all the rest. All that matters is you found it. And most importantly, you believe in it. Now, it's up to you to make it into something great.

An idea is just that - an idea. Without you it wouldn't exist. But ideas need to be put into action. They can change the smallest things or the largest problems, but none of it can happen if you don't scribble down the first one at all.
